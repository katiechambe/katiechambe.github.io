Bio

I'm a second year graduate student at the University of Arizona. I'm about to take my prelim exams, so hopefully I'll be a PhD candidate soon! I did my undergraduate degrees in physics and math at Montana State University and graduated in 2018.

I am from Montana, so I have a very deep love of the outdoors. I'm currently living in Arizona and spend most of my time on my bike, looking at mountains, or resisting the temptation to see how pointy the cacti are.
