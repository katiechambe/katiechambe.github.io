Outreach

Warrior scholar project

In the summer of 2019, I worked with the Warrior Scholar Project at the University of Arizona. I created Jupyter Notebooks that teach introductory programming concepts in python (like writing functions, making arrays, plotting). Following Notebooks then built upon those learned skills to analyze light curve data from the Kepler Space Telescope. The students learned about exoplanet dynamics and detection methods, and presented exoplanet orbital models at the end of the program.
